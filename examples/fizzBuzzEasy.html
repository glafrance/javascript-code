FizzBuzz is a coding problem often given to programming job candidates. A surprising percentage of programmers have difficulty solving this coding challenge, or take longer than expected to devise a solution, or come up with a needlessly complex solution.

Challenge - Write a program that prints the numbers from 1 to 100. But for multiples of three print "Fizz" instead of the number and for multiples of five print "Buzz". For numbers which are multiples of both three and five print "FizzBuzz".

For example: 1 2 Fizz 4 Buzz Fizz 7 8 Fizz Buzz 11 Fizz 13 14 FizzBuzz 16 17 Fizz 19 Buzz and so on...
